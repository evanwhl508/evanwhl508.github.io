What MJ Actuary Helps With

The app is designed for players who want to keep the table moving without switching between notes, calculators, and memory-heavy house-rule math.

Fast score entry

Tap a player, choose the source, set the Fan value, and save the hand. Undo remains limited to the latest hand to protect scoring integrity.

Complex rule tracking

Dealer streaks, draw behavior, jackpot pool, Pull/Kick debt, and final settlement are handled inside the app's local scoring engine.

Review after the game

History and statistics help players review the current session, including net score, win rate, deal-ins, biggest win, and streaks.
